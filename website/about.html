About Tutors Alike Inc.

We started out late 2022.

At first we were only a group of students chatting about our secondary school experinces. We noticed that we all stuggled within a certin subject and struggled to find avalible tutors in our area.

This is where the idea of Tutors Alike Inc. was born. From a simple conversation of needing extra help in school, to building a start up designed to help students recive the education they need.
